# Launching OpenDistil: Distillation-as-a-Service

**February 25, 2026 · OpenDistil Team**

---

Today, we're excited to announce the launch of **OpenDistil** — the first platform purpose-built for AI model distillation at scale.

## The Problem We're Solving

The AI industry is concentrating power in the hands of a few companies with massive compute budgets. We believe this isn't sustainable and it's not necessary.

Distillation has proven that you can reproduce frontier capabilities at a tiny fraction of the cost. But until now, running distillation pipelines required deep ML expertise, cybersecurity know-how, and significant engineering effort.

## What OpenDistil Does

We've automated the entire distillation pipeline:

1. **Connect** to any frontier model (Claude, GPT, Gemini)
2. **Extract** structured reasoning and task performance signals
3. **Build** high-quality training datasets automatically
4. **Train** optimized student models on your infrastructure
5. **Deploy** via API or private infrastructure

## Our Pricing

We've designed pricing to make distillation accessible:

- **Starter** — $5,000/run for basic distillation
- **Scale** — $25,000/run for advanced capability extraction
- **Frontier** — Custom pricing for enterprise deployments

Typical customers achieve frontier-level models for **less than 1%** of the cost of training from scratch.

## What's Next

We're just getting started. Our roadmap includes:

- Continuous distillation pipelines (keep your models updated as teachers improve)
- Specialized distillation for code, math, and reasoning
